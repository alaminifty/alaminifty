## About Me

I work at the **intersection of applied mathematics and machine learning**, with a strong focus on **understanding why models behave the way they do**, not just whether they perform well.

My approach emphasizes:
- mathematical structure and assumptions  
- interpretable and reliable modeling  
- research-driven problem solving  

---

## Research Interests

I work at the intersection of **applied mathematics and natural language processing**, with an emphasis on **retrieval-augmented generation (RAG)** for **numerically grounded reasoning over technical and scientific corpora**.

My current focus is on developing **mathematically rigorous, provenance-aware RAG systems** to enhance:

- clinical decision support in healthcare  
- advanced, analytics-driven capabilities in SaaS platforms  

I am particularly interested in building systems that combine strong theoretical foundations with **traceable, explainable, and reliable outputs** in high-stakes domains.
